История BelkaDevelopment

The history of BelkaDevelopment is quite convoluted. In 2022, the creator, who you still know today, was AmelRousAmel, but he wasn't a developer. In 2023, he was known as alexapostol10, who made games for Roblox Studio, but he never gained popularity. In 2024, he was NoodTV, and created a demo of the game "NoodTV's Love Show: How to Win a Girl". This game was based on the Catrobat programming language. The game is unfinished. There were also mods, many Minecraft mods. There was a Messenger group, which became popular with 14 supporters. In 2025, it became BelkaDevelopment, which makes games, websites, Minecraft mods, and much more.
